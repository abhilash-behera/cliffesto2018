CLIFFESTO: THE FOLDS OF ORIGAMI

Amidst the clouds, close to the soaring waters and in the heart of mighty Himalayas, awaits for you the Fiesta of the hills. Cliffesto is not a single word rather it's a combination of the words ‘Cliff’ and ‘Fest’ i.e. carnival at the hills. It is the annual technical and cultural festival of National Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand which serves as a platform to emerging and latent talents in the cultural and technical domain.
This four day long carnival is one of it's kind in the region and has become a notable event with its increasing popularity with every succeeding year. Exuberant and euphoria will induce to you and rejoice your spirits. Cultural and technical arena is open for all, along with peak entertainment. Put on your party shoes along with your brains and talent. Bring cheers this winter and let your soul be free. Fun and amusement to be ushered at Cliffesto 2018.

How to reach


By Road:
  • Srinagar (Garhwal) is located in Pauri Garhwal District, about 100 kms from Rishikesh on the road to Badrinath (NH-58)
  • NH-58 runs from New Delhi through Haridwar, Rishikesh, Devprayag to Srinagar (Garhwal) and beyond.
  • Follow NH-58 from Rishikesh to Srinagar (Garhwal); the approximate travel time from Rishikesh to Srinagar (Garhwal) is 2.5 to 3 hrs.
By Rail:
  • The last railhead en-route Srinagar is Rishikesh.
By Air:
  • Dehradun Airport located in Jollygrant is about 20 kms from Rishikesh, making the distance from Srinagar around 120 kms.
  • Prepaid taxis may be hired from the airport or a bus may be taken to Rishikesh.
